WHAT IS IT?

tejido is your new favorite networking tool

Tejido is an app designed to help recent graduates navigate the world outside of school. It can be daunting finding like-minded individuals once you're no longer in the constant social college environment.
Tejido aims to connect those in similar fields and direct them to activities and events that are relevant to their studies.
