WHAT IS IT?

tejido is your new favorite networking tool

Tejido is an app designed to help recent graduates navigate the world outside of school. It can be daunting finding like-minded individuals once you're no longer in the constant social college environment.
Tejido aims to connect those in similar fields and direct them to activities and events that are relevant to their studies.

FEATURES

Tejido combines the easy communication of dating apps with the professional connections of platforms like LinkedIn to create an app that offers students a quick and easy means of finding like minded individuals. It will feature a "connect" page, which can be customized to offer networking opportunities based on your major, or just your personal interests.
Each user will create his or her own personal profile, listing their interests, and what they mean to gain from the app, whether that be a new study buddy, or a plus-one to a local jazz concert. They may include pictures of themselves, links to social media, and any other personal or professional information they wish to disclose. There will be an "inbox"
page that allows users to communicate with people they've bookmarked as potential matches. It will have one-on-one messaging as well as group chat capabilities for organizing larger events. Profiles are color-coded according to the user's major.

DESIGN
